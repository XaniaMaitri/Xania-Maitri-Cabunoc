Self-Introduction

Sup! My name's Xania Maitri Espinosa Cabunoc - pretty unique nooo? I let people just call me ‘Xania/Xan’ but 'Maimai' akong ipatawag if they’re close friends of mine. I'm 18 years-old and was born on July 9, 2005. I like to dance, draw, sing, create art, read, exercise, and play the guitar. Currently, I'm learning graphic and multimedia arts. I'm looking forward in learning more, not just in that, but a lot of things that fuels and fills my curiosity!

Me in 8 years?

Back when I was a small kid while growing up, my parents and other family members have always encouraged me to be like my 2 uncles, both well-rounded PMA (Philippine Military Academy) Officials who excel in their fields. As a child, I saw myself wearing their uniforms and walking in their shoes but didn't quite understand why that is until lately when my mind turned more mature. I am now in my years of self-exploration and a big part of myself sees the future life I am currently working on. In terms of starting my own family, I see myself being married but with no children. I am looking forward to owning pets though. I see myself traveling the world, discovering different places, and generally go on adventures. I see myself feeling fulfilled and happy because I choose to follow what my heart desires.
